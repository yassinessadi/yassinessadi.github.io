Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the SkyStream application (“SkyStream”, “the app”, “we”) handles information when you use it. SkyStream is an IPTV media player for Android. It does not provide, host, or sell any television channels or media content — you supply your own playlist or stream link.

Information we collect

SkyStream is designed to work without an account and without collecting personal information on our own servers. Specifically:

  • Sources you add — M3U URLs, Xtream Codes credentials (server, username, password), and stream links are stored locally on your device so the app can load and re-load them. They are not transmitted to us.
  • App preferences — favorites, watch history, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not collect your name, email, contacts, photos, or precise location, and we do not require you to register.

Streaming and third-party sources

When you load a playlist or play a stream, your device connects directly to the third-party servers you provide. Those providers may receive your IP address and standard request information under their own privacy policies. SkyStream has no control over, and is not responsible for, the content or practices of those providers.

Advertising

SkyStream may display ads provided by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, advertising partners may use device identifiers and similar technologies. You can learn more in Google’s advertising policy, and you can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings. The app-ads.txt file on this domain authorizes our advertising sellers.

Analytics and crash reporting

The app may use standard Google Play services and crash-reporting tools to keep the app stable. These may collect limited, non-identifying technical data such as device model, OS version, and crash logs. This data is used only to maintain and improve the app.

Children

SkyStream is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • Clear your saved sources, favorites, and history at any time from within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • Manage ad personalization through your device's advertising settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
