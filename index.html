What it does

Built for the way you actually watch

Three ways in, one clean library out — plus the tools to keep it tidy when sources go stale.

Three ways to add a source

Drop in a direct M3U playlist URL, sign in with Xtream Codes (server, username, password), or paste a single stream link to watch instantly — no playlist required.

Stream Health checker

Test every channel at once and see real latency in milliseconds, with online and offline flagged at a glance.

Search 12,000+ channels

Instant search and category filters — Favorites, Shop, News, General and more — across your whole loaded library.

Favorites & history

Star the channels you keep coming back to and pick up right where you left off with continue watching.

Sources that stay put

Working sources are saved and verified, so a playlist you trusted last week is one tap away today.

Stream formats it speaks

Paste any of these and SkyStream plays it directly.
